# Affinity Tax Services — Logo & Usage Guidelines

## Logo Family

Primary (icon + text): `public/brand/affinity-primary.svg`

## Clear Space

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the capital “A” around all sides of the logo. No text, graphics, or UI elements should enter this area.

## Minimum Sizes

- Primary logo: 220 px width (web), 35 mm (print)
- Text-only logo: 180 px width (web), 30 mm (print)
- Favicon/icon: 32×32 px (PNG), 180×180 px (Apple touch)

## Usage Rules

- Do not alter proportions or rearrange icon/text.
- Do not change colors outside the palette.
- Ensure contrast meets WCAG AA against backgrounds.
- Use B&W versions for one-color printing or emboss/deboss.

## File Formats

- Vector: SVG (preferred for all digital and print)
- Raster exports: PNG @ 1×, 2×, 3× (generate as needed from SVG)

## Placement

- Header or footer preferred; maintain clear space and minimum size.
- On dark backgrounds, use the primary logo; on very light backgrounds, ensure adequate contrast.

## Trademark & Originality
